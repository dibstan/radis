Vespa BM25 Help

Vespa ist a modern full text search database and BM25 is a simple search ranking algorithm based on the query terms appearing in each document.

{# https://docs.vespa.ai/en/reference/simple-query-language-reference.html #} The following rules apply:

  • Search is case-insensitive.
  • All terms in the query must be matched by a document (implicit AND query).
  • term1 OR term2 (capital OR) means that either term1 or term2 must match.
  • Terms surrounded by quotes are search as exact phrase, e.g. "lung disease".
  • + in front of a term means that the exact term must match.
